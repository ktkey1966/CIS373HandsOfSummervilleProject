Community Service

The Summerville community is all about coming together to make our neighborhoods better-whether it's cleaning up parks, organizing a food drive, or helping at local events. Volunteering with Helping Hands of Summerville is a way to connect with others, give back, and feel proud of the difference you're making. Together, we can create a stronger, more vibrant community!

Education & Mentorship

At Helping Hands of Summerville, we empower the next generation through our volunteer education and mentorship programs. Share your knowledge and experience to guide and inspire individuls to achieve their goals both academically and professionally. Make a lasting impact by becoming a volunteer mentor or educator today!

Environmental Conservation

Protect the Summerville community, our planet, and its resources by becoming a Helping Hands of Summerville environmental conservationist volunteer. Contribute to hands-on projects, educate others, and advocate for sustainable practices. Be a difference maker, volunteer to preserve nature today for tomorrow's generations!

Healthcare & Support

Our healthcare volunteers provide support, comfort, and care to those in need. We offer a helping hand and compassionate presence to patients, families, and healthcare professionals. You can make a meaningful difference in the lives of others during challenging times.

Disaster Relief & Emergency Services

Help your community recover and rebuild by volunteering with Helping Hands of Summerville, disaster relief and emergency services. Provide vital support during crises, from immediate response to long-term recovery efforts. Be a beacon of hope and resilience in times of need.

Animal Welfare

Give a voice to the voiceless by volunteering with Helping Hands of Summerville, animal welfare services. Provide compassionate care, assist with adoptions, and promote responsible pet ownership. You will help to create a Summeville community where animals are safe, loved, and thriving.
